Calorie counter

Calorie counter is an app that lets a user stay on top of his or her fitness goals by providing a convenient tool to log their calorie intake throughout the the day . Just enter your info and create a fitness plan , or select one . Enter your meals as your consume them and the app will keep track of your targets over the duration of the meal plan .

Research findings

There is an increasing problem of obesity and being over weight over the years. An app that lets users track their calorie intake over a duration of time can help them in their weight management goals .
