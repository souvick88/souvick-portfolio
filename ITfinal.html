Insta-travel

Insta travel is an app that lets a user book a vacation to any of the wide variety of available locations without having to deal with the hassle of taking care of every aspect from planning the trip to booking tickets . Browse through the many destinations available and check out the pictures and reviews left by previous users who have taken that vacation . Just select where you wish to go and have no need to worry about the dozens of different decisions that will be taken care of by the app.

Research conclusions

  • Research shows that people are increasingly turning to their cell phones to research travel destinations.
  • People are looking for destinations to vacation at over the weekends .A travel app that lets users book vacations spontaneously will lead to greater conversions from those researching destinations to those booking vacations.
