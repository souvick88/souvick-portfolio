VR art gallery

VR art gallery is a VR based app that lets the user have the same experience as visiting a art gallery or a museum within the comfort of their homes . Users can view the artworks of their choice within an immersive environment and learn interesting facts and trivia about it in an interactive manner.

Research conclusions

The market for VR is booming . An app that can bring the museum or gallery experience to the user by tapping into the potential of VR to create an immersive and engaging setting can have a huge demand for itself.

Game developer

“I am an amateur artist and have a passion for paintings . I would love for there to be an app that lets me experience a work of art without having to make a visit to the gallery or a museum. ”
